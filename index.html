What is Geometry Dash?


Geometry Dash is a rhythm-based platformer game that was released in 2013. The original premise of the game is that you are a player who must avoid obstacles in order to get to the end of the level. However, using the in-game level editor, players have created levels that can only be described as works of art. This service provides a way to showcase your own masterpieces in 4K UHD quality and vibrant HDR colors.
